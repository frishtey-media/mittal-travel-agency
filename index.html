Our Services

Mittal Travel Agency is a conglomerate that offers a range of top-notch services, including Air Tickets, Holiday Packages and Money Transfers Worldwide.

AIR TICKETS

Let us know what you need in order for us to book a ticket and we can get it booked for you. We’re able to provide tickets for both and domestic and international level because we have tie-ups with almost all the airlines across the globe, enabling us to offer tickets at cheap price. You can book tickets based on travel duration, rates and offers or airline, all we guarantee is a hassle-free ticket booking at the best rates.

HOLIDAY PACKAGES

With our special holiday packages, you can select the ideal destination both domestically in India and beyond. These customized vacation packages provide a variety of excursions that include popular activities in addition to the obligatory sightseeing, lodging, food, and spa services, among other amenities. Our holiday packages are tailored to your desired likes and preferences which results in making memorable vacation.

TRAVEL INSURANCE

We make sure you have the best travel insurance coverage for unplanned medical and non-medical emergencies while you're away from home. You can select from any of the top travel insurance plans to ensure that your international trip is secure and protected from unanticipated circumstances. Whether you are traveling for business or pleasure, we have the greatest packages that will meet your needs at incredibly low prices.

RAILWAY TICKETS

Nothing can beat the joy of travelling on a train. It is one of the best-connected means of transportation in our country and one of the cheapest as well. Nowadays, you can find many Railway Ticketing Agents who can help you book railway tickets. So, if you love train journeys or don’t want to spend on airfare then we can book tickets for you. We’re a retailer of TRIP JACK PRIVATE LIMITED and is hereby authorized to book Railway E-tickets as per Rail E-Ticketing terms & conditions using IRCTC Certificate number WTRIPJK02218.

PASSPORT APPLY

A passport is a very important document that is essential not only for international travel but also accepted as identity proof in many legal transactions. Gone are the days when you were required to stand in long queue to apply for a passport. With the advent of technology, you can apply for a passport through Mittal Travel Agency without standing in the long queue. we have more than 7 years of experience in this field.

RECEIVE PAYMENT GLOBALLY

Mittal Travel Agency facilitates seamless outward remittances through Western Union, RIA, and MoneyGram, ensuring swift transfers globally. With user-friendly software and stringent verification processes, funds are securely transmitted within minutes. Required documentation includes one ID proof, passport-sized photo, and PAN card. Our service guarantees low costs, rapid transfers, transparency, and accessibility in major currencies worldwide.

OUTWARD REMITTANCES/WIRE TRANSFER

The most popular and straightforward method of transferring money abroad is through outward remittances, often known as SWIFT transfers. The hassle-free overseas remittance service offered by Mittal Travel Agency features the lowest costs, minimum time, total transparency, and ease of use. Worldwide, the outward remittance facility and SWIFT transfers are accessible in all major currencies.

INDO CANADIAN BUS COMPANY

We're an Authorised ticket seller of Indo Canadian Bus Booking in Bathinda. We don't charge any extra amount to book tickets.Contact us to book ticket to/from Delhi Airport.
